## For the Multi-Location Pet Care Operator

### The Problem You Know

Your safety documentation is a mess.

Incident reports live in email. OSHA logs are spreadsheets that might be wrong. When something serious happens, your team spends days pulling together documentation that should take minutes. When an OSHA inspector shows up, you're hoping nothing got lost.

You also know there are patterns you're not seeing. The same kennel keeps having issues. The same shift. Maybe even the same dog. But you can't connect the dots because your data is scattered.

### What PackGuardian Does

**For your field staff:**
Report incidents from a phone in under 60 seconds. No complicated forms. Just speak or type what happened — the platform handles the rest. Works without WiFi.

**For your safety coordinator (or whoever handles that):**
OSHA recordability is determined automatically. Form 300 logs update themselves. Corrective actions are tracked with due dates and owners. Nothing falls through the cracks.

**For you:**
Every location's safety health in one view. Which centers have unresolved follow-up actions. Which centers have recurring incidents. Which centers are escalating. All without chasing reports.

### The Risk You're Carrying Without It

**OSHA penalties** for failing to record or report: up to $15,625 per violation, $156,259 for willful violations.

**Workers' compensation** claims that are poorly documented cost 35–60% more to settle than well-documented ones.

**Liability** from dog bites and animal incidents where the investigation trail is incomplete.

**Reputation risk** when incidents repeat because no corrective action was tracked.

None of these are hypothetical for a 10-location pet care operation. They happen. The question is whether you have a system that protects you when they do.
