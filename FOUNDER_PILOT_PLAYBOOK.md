## The 5-Minute Pitch

### The Hook (30 seconds)
*"If OSHA showed up at your busiest location tomorrow, how long would it take you to show them your incident records, your corrective actions, and your current year 300 log?"*

Pause. Listen.

Then: *"PackGuardian means that answer is 'two minutes.'"*

### The Problem (60 seconds)
*"Pet care operations deal with real physical risk every day. Dogs bite. Staff get hurt. Things happen. But most operations document incidents in paper forms, email chains, and spreadsheets that are disorganized, incomplete, or lost.*

*At the same time, OSHA has specific requirements — the 300 log, the 301 forms, the annual posting — that most pet care operators aren't meeting correctly. Not because they don't care. Because they don't have the right tools."*

### The Solution (60 seconds)
*"PackGuardian is the operational safety layer built specifically for pet care. Three things that matter:*

*One — field staff report incidents from their phone in 60 seconds, voice-first, works offline.*

*Two — OSHA compliance happens automatically. The system determines recordability, populates the 300 log, tracks your documentation.*

*Three — you can see which of your locations need attention today, not next Monday when someone emails you a report."*

### The Demo Hook (30 seconds)
*"Let me show you a dog bite being reported in 45 seconds, and then I'll show you what your safety director sees when it happens."*

**→ Go to live demo**

### The Close (30 seconds)
*"Under $50 per location per month. No IT required. 30 minutes to set up a new location. If a pilot doesn't show you value in 30 days, we end it — no questions."*

## Objection Handling (Quick Reference)

**"It's too expensive."**  
→ "Under $50/location/month. One documented workers' comp claim improvement more than pays for two years."

**"My staff won't use it."**  
→ "Voice entry, no typing required. Faster than paper. If they're not using it in 30 days, we fix the UX — that's our problem, not yours."

**"We already track this in spreadsheets."**  
→ "How long does it take to compile the year-end OSHA 300 log? Can you tell me right now which locations have open corrective actions?"

**"We don't really have OSHA issues."**  
→ "Have you been audited? A dog bite requiring stitches is recordable. A back strain from lifting is recordable. Most operators don't know their exposure until it's too late."

**"I need to involve my legal team."**  
→ "Absolutely. Ask them specifically about 29 CFR 1904 compliance and whether your current process creates audit exposure. That's the conversation we're built for."

## What NOT to Promise

| Don't Say | Say Instead |
|-----------|-------------|
| "We'll prevent dog bites" | "We help you document, investigate, and prevent recurrence" |
| "OSHA-certified software" | "Automates OSHA 300/301/300A workflows" |
| "AI-powered compliance" | "AI-assisted intake with rule-based OSHA determination" |
| "Zero incidents guaranteed" | "Better visibility into patterns before they escalate" |
| "Eliminate your OSHA liability" | "Improve your documentation trail and recordability accuracy" |
| "We'll customize it for you" | "We'll configure terminology and org structure — the core workflow is standard" |
| "Enterprise-grade security" | "Tenant-isolated, audit-trailed, access-controlled" |

## Strongest Operational Value Points (in order of impact)

1. **OSHA recordability automatic** — Removes the guesswork that causes under-reporting or over-reporting
2. **60-second mobile intake** — Removes the documentation delay that corrupts incident records
3. **Cross-location pattern detection** — Surfaces recurring hazards before they become serious
4. **Corrective action tracking** — Closes the loop that paper forms never do
5. **Investigation trail** — Creates defensible documentation for claims, audits, and legal

## Pricing Confidence

You should be confident saying:

- **$49/location/month** for 1–5 locations
- **$39/location/month** for 6–15 locations
- **$29/location/month** for 16+ locations
- **Free 90-day pilot** before any payment
- **Month-to-month** after pilot, cancel anytime

**At 10 locations:** $390/month = $4,680/year  
**One well-documented WC claim improvement** at even 10% reduction = $4,000–$6,000 savings  
**Break-even:** Less than one claim.

Don't apologize for the pricing. It's priced below value.

## Managing Expectations for the Pilot

**Be explicit with pilot operators:**

- This is real software but early-stage — there will be rough edges
- We will respond to bugs within 24 hours, same-day for blockers
- Your feedback directly shapes the product
- We will not sell you a feature that doesn't exist yet
- The pilot is 90 days; we'll both know by day 30 whether it's working

**The pilot "wins" when:**
- At least 10 real incidents reported by field staff (not admin)
- At least 1 corrective action tracked to completion
- Internal champion logging in weekly
- Operator says "I'd recommend this to another kennel"
