# PackGuardian — Pilot Program Overview

## Program Summary

PackGuardian is accepting 3–5 pilot operators for a structured 90-day pilot. Pilot participants get full platform access, direct founder support, and significant input into product direction. In return, we ask for honest feedback and permission to reference your experience in future sales conversations (with your approval on any specific claims).

---

## What Pilot Participants Get

**Full platform access**
- All features: incident reporting, case management, OSHA automation, corrective actions, witness statements, pattern detection, executive reporting
- Up to 25 locations configured
- Unlimited users

**Direct support**
- Dedicated Slack/email channel with the founding team
- Weekly check-in call (optional, 30 minutes)
- Same-day response to bugs or questions
- Custom terminology support (call incidents what your team calls them)

**Pricing**
- 90-day pilot: free
- After pilot: first 3 months at 50% of standard pricing if you continue

**Product influence**
- Monthly product roadmap review — you see what we're building and why
- Feature requests from pilot participants jump the queue
- Named acknowledgment in the product if desired

---

## What We Ask For

- 1 designated internal champion (typically a safety coordinator, operations manager, or owner)
- At least 10 real incidents reported through the platform during the pilot
- Monthly 30-minute feedback call with the founding team
- A written or verbal reference we can use with future customers (pending your approval on specifics)

## Pilot Timeline

### Month 1 — Adoption
**Goal:** System live, team using it for real incidents

Week 1:
- Platform configured for your locations
- Admin and manager accounts created
- 15-minute team onboarding (can be recorded for future staff)
- First incident submitted

Week 2–4:
- Regular incident reporting established
- First corrective actions created
- First OSHA log review

Month 1 check-in: What's working, what's confusing, what's missing

### Month 2 — Integration
**Goal:** Platform becoming part of normal workflow

- OSHA log populated with real data
- Corrective actions tracked through to completion
- Pattern detection findings reviewed
- Location health scores established

Month 2 check-in: ROI conversation — what would this have cost without PackGuardian?

### Month 3 — Depth
**Goal:** Platform demonstrably improving safety operations

- Executive briefing reviewed with leadership
- Year-to-date OSHA compliance status confirmed
- Recurring incident patterns addressed
- Post-pilot decision: continue, expand, or end

## Ideal Pilot Participant Profile

**You're a great fit if:**
- You have 3–25 pet care locations (kennels, daycares, grooming, boarding, vet boarding)
- You've had a workers' comp claim, OSHA inspection, or serious incident in the past 2 years
- You have someone who can champion internal adoption (doesn't need to be technical)
- You're frustrated with your current process (paper, email, Google Forms)
- You're open to giving candid feedback even when things aren't perfect

**You're not a great fit if:**
- You're a single-location owner-operator with no staff
- You need an enterprise procurement process before getting started
- You want a "set it and forget it" tool without any internal champion

## Questions We Get

**"What if our staff won't use it?"**  
The mobile intake takes under 60 seconds and supports voice entry. It's faster than any paper form or email. Adoption resistance usually comes from clunky tools — this isn't one.

**"What happens to our data if we don't continue?"**  
You can export all your incident and case data at any time. We don't hold data hostage.

**"Is this HIPAA / OSHA / SOC2 compliant?"**  
OSHA workflows follow 29 CFR 1904 requirements. SOC2 audit is planned for 2026. For employee medical data, visibility controls ensure only authorized users (HR role) can see sensitive fields.

**"Do you integrate with [ADP / BambooHR / our kennel management software]?"**  
Not yet in pilot, but our API is open and webhook events are available for custom integrations.
