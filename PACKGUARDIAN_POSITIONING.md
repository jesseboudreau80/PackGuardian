# PackGuardian — Product Positioning

## One-Line

**Operational safety intelligence for multi-location pet care.**

## The 30-Second Version

PackGuardian is the safety operations platform built specifically for pet care businesses. Field staff report incidents from their phone in under 60 seconds — voice-first, mobile-first, works offline. The platform automatically determines OSHA recordability, creates an investigation case, tracks corrective actions, and surfaces recurring risk patterns across locations. Safety coordinators and executives get portfolio-level visibility without chasing spreadsheets.

## What Makes It Different

Most incident management software is built for manufacturing or construction — large organizations with dedicated EHS departments, safety officers, and enterprise budgets.

Pet care operators have none of that. They have:
- Kennel technicians, groomers, and shift supervisors who are too busy to use complicated software
- Managers covering 3–15 locations without a safety coordinator
- OSHA obligations they don't fully understand
- Recurring incidents they can't see because there's no data

PackGuardian is built for how pet care actually works — not how enterprise safety software assumes it works.

## The Value Hierarchy

### 1. Compliance Protection (immediate)
- OSHA 300/301/300A automated

- OSHA posting reminders
- "Am I compliant?" becomes answerable

### 2. Operational Confidence (30-90 days)
- Every incident documented with proper chain of custody
- Corrective actions tracked to completion
- Cases never fall through the cracks
- "What happened to that dog bite from last month?" becomes answerable

### 3. Pattern Intelligence (90+ days)
- Recurring risks visible across locations
- "Which locations need attention today?" becomes answerable
- "Why does this keep happening at FL-MIA?" becomes answerable
- Predictive risk scoring by center

## Pricing Philosophy

PackGuardian should be priced per location, not per seat.

Pet care operators think in locations. A kennel owner with 5 locations doesn't think "I have 40 staff members." They think "I have 5 facilities."

Suggested structure:
- $49/month per location (1-5 locations)
- $39/month per location (6-15 locations)
- $29/month per location (16+ locations)
- OSHA compliance module included
- No per-seat limits for field staff

At $49/month per location, a 5-location operator pays $245/month — less than one workers' comp claim's paperwork costs to handle manually.

## What We Are Not Saying

We do NOT position PackGuardian as:
- "AI-powered" (misleading — most features are rule-based with optional AI enhancement)
- "OSHA compliant" (we automate OSHA workflows, but compliance is the operator's responsibility)
- "Reduce accidents" (we improve visibility and documentation, not physical safety itself)
- Enterprise EHS software (we're deliberately not that)

We DO say:
- "Purpose-built for pet care operations"
- "OSHA-ready from day one"
- "Incident to investigation in under 60 seconds"
- "Visibility across all your locations"

## Target Customer Profile

**Ideal:** Regional pet care chain, 5–25 locations, privately owned or PE-backed, has had at least one OSHA inspection or workers' comp claim that was painful to document.

**Secondary:** Franchise group or cooperative of independent kennels looking for standardized safety practices.

**Not ideal (yet):** Single-location owner-operator without staff.
