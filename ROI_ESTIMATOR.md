## Notes for the Sales Conversation

- Don't overclaim. These are estimates, not guarantees.
- The WC benefit is the most compelling for operators who've had a serious claim.
- The documentation time savings is easiest to verify in the first 30 days of pilot.
- Focus on the operator's most recent painful experience — that's where they feel the ROI.
